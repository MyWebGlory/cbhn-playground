Featured Speaker
Dr. Monica Soni
Chief Medical Officer, Covered California

A Harvard-trained internist and “agitator for change,” Dr. Soni has spent two decades at the crossroads of medicine, social justice, and health policy. Before becoming CMO at Covered California, she led specialty care across LA County’s public health system, the second-largest in the nation. She is also an Associate Professor at UCLA and Charles R. Drew University.
